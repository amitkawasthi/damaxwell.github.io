Travel Information

Fairbanks has an international airport minutes from the university and is served by direct flights from the lower 48 states as well as some direct flights in the summer from Europe (e.g. Condor Airlines).

Although Fairbanks has a public transit system, service is limited and does not permit exploring the surrounding region. Uber just started in Alaska, and there are a number of taxi services. For participants staying at Pike's Waterfront Lodge, there will be a daily shuttle between the university and the hotel. Particpants may find it convenient to rent a car for other tourism.

Tourism destinations in the region include

  • University of Alaska Museum of the North
  • Chena Hot Springs
  • Manley Hot Springs
  • Denali National Park
  • Morris Thompson Cultural and Visitors Center

Other visitor resources are available from Explore Fairbanks.
